The history of HTML dates back to the early 1990s when it was first developed by Tim Berners-Lee. Over the years, HTML has evolved significantly, with numerous versions and updates that have introduced new features and capabilities. The introduction of HTML5 brought about a major overhaul, adding support for multimedia elements, improved semantics, and enhanced accessibility features.
